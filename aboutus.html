From the Creators of Talkipy

It was at the beginning of 2020 that the pandemic and isolation created the dire need for a safe space for people to talk and open up. Stuck in their houses many felt stranded and alone. Even with people around, opening up to a non-judgemental ear seemed impossible.
It seemed important to us, to let them know that there are people who care. With this motive, we started off on this platform.
Our team who have continuously been the pillar of support, includes young psychologists, social workers, and social scientists from prestigious institutions.

- Gayatri and Khushi (Co-Founders, Talkipy)

Graduates from Tata Institute of Social Sciences

Vision

A world where mental health care and support are accessible and affordable to all.

Mission

Providing early-stage interventions, spreading awareness, and precisely linking available mental health services to the people in need.

Our core values

Compassion

We at Talkipy believe that compassion comes from understanding the problem. And the biggest act of compassion is being there for someone in their time of need.

Persistence

We as a team, are built with a never-give-up attitude, We will keep working towards our goals through thick and thin and will continue to strive towards them.

Learning

Learning never reaches a saturation point and so throughout our mental health journey we hope to learn, unlearn and relearn along with everyone.

Impact

Big or small, the little happiness, comfort and support that we are able to create through our work is most valuable to us.
