What is Talkipy

We've all dealt with issues that we were never able to speak about, we've all had times when we wanted to share something but had no one who would listen...
So Talkipy is our solution for you!! Our initiative is a platform where you can find a person to talk, vent, and let go of those bottled-up emotions.
There is more! We also provide you with customized sessions with professionals and help you find the most suitable therapist according to your requirements because after all your mental health is our priority!!!

What We Bring

Free Chat

An anonymous, instant chat platform, where you will find non-judgemental friends to open up to

Book a Session

A scheduled session prepared according to the needs of our client over chat, or voice call

A suitable therapist

Finding it difficult to find a professional psychologist that suits your needs. We got you covered

Our Motto

Our intention is to create a safe space that will help you heal better.

Support

Still Doubtful

Your privacy is our first and foremost concern.
We understand how difficult it is to open up and speak about those troubling thoughts. That is why we are presenting you a safe space, where all your information will remain confidential and your identity anonymous. It's only you in a safe place with a trustworthy friend.
